About the Temple

Shri Vitthal Rukmini Temple in Pandharpur is one of the most sacred pilgrimage sites in Maharashtra. Dedicated to Lord Vitthal, an incarnation of Vishnu, and his consort Rukmini, this temple attracts millions of devotees every year, especially during the Ashadi Ekadashi festival.
